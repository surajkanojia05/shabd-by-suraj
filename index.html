Some ghazal written by Suraj Kanojia

Khafa hai

Mere har shabd me parchaai jiski
Vo bhi aajkal kuch khafa hai,
Haan vo bas zara naraaz hai
Me kyu kahu zamane se, ki woh bewafa hai

Ek Khwaab

Mere jazbaaton ke samandar me, ek khwab chl raha h
khayalon ki koi manzil nahi, vo khwab chl raha h
Iss bejaan jahan me, bas ek khwab chl raha h
Sab kuch ruka hua h mera, par ek khwab chl raha h
Soya nahi hoon barson se me, par ek khwab chl raha h
Aankhe laal hain neend ki khwahish.... me,
Phir bhi ek khwab chl raha h
Ek khwab chl raha h.

Dikhta khush Zarur hoon

Vakt chal raha hai, par saanse tham si gayi hai
Muskura to rha hu mai, par aankhe nam si gayi hai
Dikhta khush zarur hoon magar,
Dikhta khush zarur hoon magar
Jeene ki khwaahish abb ho, kam si gayi hai

Raat Bhar

Mohabbat Mohabbat karte rahe raat bhar hum,
Uski yaadon ko apne zehan me pirote rahe raat bhar hum,
Aur vo shaks aaya aur hume bewafa bata gaya

Mulaqaat ho naa ho

Kya pata ab fir mulaqaat ho naa ho
Kya pata ab fir woh haseen baat ho naa ho
Par har raat tujhe khwabon me hum yaad karte rahenge
Tu aaye yaa naa aaye hum jab tak rahenge intezaar karte rahenge.

Tera vapas aana

Tera vapas aana, naa aana vo alag baat hai
Dekh kar muh ghumana, ye to galat baat hai

Khush kaun hai

Ye begaani si duniya me akhir apna kaun hai,
Agar sab hi pareshaan hain, to khush kaun hai
